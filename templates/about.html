How We Do It

Our platform utilizes a robust machine learning model trained on a vast dataset of symptoms and diseases. By inputting your symptoms, our system generates accurate predictions about potential illnesses, allowing you to make informed decisions about your health.

Important: How to Use HealthSage Correctly

To ensure that you receive the most accurate health insights, it’s essential to input your symptoms correctly:

  • Be Specific and Accurate: When entering symptoms, make sure to use precise medical terms or select from our dropdown menu to avoid any confusion.
  • Correct Spelling: Incorrect or misspelled symptoms can lead to inaccurate predictions. Please double-check your inputs to ensure they match the options provided by our system.
  • Select Relevant Symptoms: Choose only the symptoms that you are currently experiencing. Including unrelated symptoms may skew the results.

Remember:

The accuracy of our analysis depends heavily on the quality of the input. Please take a moment to review your symptoms before submitting to get the best possible recommendations.
